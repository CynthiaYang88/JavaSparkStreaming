About our Project

"NY State Oil Production" is a project developed by Revature's April 13rd, 2020 Big Data Batch. The purpose of our application is to use all the concepts learned throughout the training program to build an automated big data streaming pipeline. Our data source consists of oil and gas information from New York State Department of Environmental Conservation Division of Mineral Resources, and our main goal is to provide a visual representation in real time of the history of a wellbore's production of oil, gas, or water during its lifecycle. We will provide a Map view of the state of New York and some interesting findings displayed in graphs.
